1. Educate children.Education is one of the best solutions to poverty. 2. Provide clean water. 3. Ensure basic health care. 4. Empower a girl or women. 5. Support environmental programs.

How poor is india 2023?

The 2023 Multidimensional poverty index Report finds over a third of all poor people in the world live in south Asia-Which is around 389 million people.India contributes signficantly to this number,accounting for almost 70 percent of the increase in extreme poverty.
